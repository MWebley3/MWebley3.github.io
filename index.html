Recent Projects

Python Project - Prosper Loan Data Exploration

The objective of this project is to provide valuable guidance for stakeholders in optimizing lending strategies, risk assessment, and customer engagement. Analysis of Prosper Loan lending services using pandas, NumPy, and data visualization tools, Seaborn and Matplotlib, was performed to gain a comprehensive understanding of Prosper Loan lending services' performance and customer trends.

TaTa Forage Job Stimulation: Global Mart 2011 Sales Dashboard

The primary aim of this stimulation was to use the optimal data visualization methods for the CEO and CMO of Global Mart, empowering them to sustain their upward sales trajectory using historical data. Data was provided by TaTa and cleaned using Microsoft Excel and visualized using Tableau providing clarity and actionable insight.

Excel Visualization: CentralEmporium July 2021 Sales Dashboard

The goal of this project was to gain insights on consumer preferences, product demand dynamics, and regional market trends. The sales data encompassed transactions from two distinct countries, South Africa and Nigeria. Sales data was analyzed using Microsoft Excel to glean and present actionable insights to inform decision makers on market trends in July 2021 for CentralEmporium.

Who Done It? A SQL Murder Mystery

This project highlights the power of data-driven investigation techniques in criminal analysis and forensic science. Leveraging advanced SQL techniques such as JOINs and filter methods, including WHERE and HAVING clauses, enabled the meticulous identification of the suspect. Additionally, employing the LIKE operator allowed for more nuanced analysis, unveiling intricate clues and connections crucial to unraveling the mystery.

PowerBI Visualization: KMS Superstore Sales Dashboard

This PowerBI dashboard provides an insightful overview of KMS Superstore sales spanning from 2009 to 2012, offering a comprehensive analysis of regional profits, customer segment profits, and more. The interactive dashboard equips stakeholders with the clarity and foresight needed to drive business growth and maximize profitability.

Accenture Forage Job Stimulation: Social Buzz Content Analysis

The analysis aimed to provide actionable insights to stakeholders by examining the top five content categories based on Social Buzz's popularity score, to identify and optimize algorithmic trends. The dataset, provided by Accenture, underwent cleaning in Microsoft Excel and transformation using PowerQuery. The resulting insights were effectively presented in PowerBI, informing strategic decisions and enhancing performance, to drive business success.
